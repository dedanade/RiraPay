Why RiraPay?

Wondering why RiraPay is Better than other means of payment you've been using? Go through the list below and be the judge.

Fast Shipping

When you buy with Rirapay, the merchants has 48 hours to ship the item and provide a tracking number which will be verified. You're eligible to request your money back immediately the 48 hours elapsed.

Easy Tracking

Always wondering when you will receive your orders? We will be working with top logistics firm so you easily track all orders through your rirapay account and know where your item currently is and when it will be delivered.

7 Days Money-Back-Gurantee

Have you had the experience of paying for an item on delivery and later on realise the product doesn't work as described but you can't get your money back? Well, with RiraPay, you enjoy 7 days Money-back Guarantee. Immediately a product/services is delivered to you, you have 7 days to test the quality of the product. If you feel cheated or product doesn't fit the description, you can request your money back with one click on a button!



Instant Messaging

Experiencing slow response from merchants after paying for a product? With Rirapay, you can chat with your merchants directly on the platform and also request your money back if the merchant ignores your message.

Fast Return Process

When you request a refund for a product, the merchant has 24 hours to reject or accept your request. If rejected, you can escalate the refund process to Rirapay Resolution Team who will respond within 24 hours. If you your refund is approved, the money is sent back to your wallet immediately!

How Does RiraPay Work?

Wondering how RiraPay works? RiraPay works in the following steps:

  • Sign up on Rirapay

    • Click on the Get Started Button and register with your real details


  • Request for an Invoice

    • Whenever you're shopping online, request for a Rirapay Invoice OR watchout for Rirapay as a method of payment on your favuorite e-commerce store.


  • Make Payment

    • Make payment through our secured and certified payment proccesor. We hold this payment till order has been delivered and tested for 7 days after the delivered date.


  • Wait for Shipment

    • After payment, wait for 48 hours for your product to be ship with a tracking number. If product is not shipped within 48 hours, you're eligible to resuest your money back which will be refunded immediately.


  • Product Delivered

    • When your product is delivered, you want to check and make sure it fits the description; from size, to color and other description you can easily indentify. If it doesn't fit description, you go back to the transaction page to request for your money back and also inlcude pictures/videos why you're requesting for money back; don't worry if you're not sure of the quality, you have 7 days more to check the quality of the product.


  • Refund Process

    • We run the fastest refund process ever! When you request for a refund, the merchant receives the notification and must respond to your request within 24 hours. If merchant accept your request, the funds is automically sent back to your Rirapay wallet which you can withdraw into your bank account anytime of the day. If your request is rejected by the merchant, and you're not satisfied with the reasons given, you can then escalate the case to Rirapay Resolution Team who will investigate and respond with a verdict within 24 hours!
