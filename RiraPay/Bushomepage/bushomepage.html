What is RiraPay for Business?

RiraPay for Business is an escrow payment infrastructure that serves as an intermidiary between your business and your customers. Rirapay makes sure your products is delivered timely with zero percent cancelation rate at the same time make sure your customers are satisfied.

Why RiraPay for Business?

Inventory Management

Not sure when your stock is going to finish or when you should stock up? With our inventory management, you can manage all your inventories and also add an alert when your stock is going low according to your settings.

How Does RiraPay for Business Work?

Wondering how RiraPay for Business works? RiraPay works in the following steps:

  • Sign up on Rirapay

    • Click on the Get Started Button and register with your real bsuiness details


  • Create a payment link

    • Insert the payment link to your ecommerce store or if you're a social commerce merchant, reconmend RiraPay to your customers and send the payment link to make payment.


  • Recieve Payment

    • Payment is made through our secured and certified payment proccesor. Once payment has been made, you will receive a notification on your mail and you can confirm the payment on your transaction page on RiraPay web app.


  • Ship Product

    • After payment has been confirmed, you have 48 hours to ship the product or order might be canceled by the buyer. If you're not able to ship product within the 48 hours timefraame, send a message through the instant messaging and plead for delivery time extension. when shipped, mark the order as shipped and include the logistics firm and tracking number


  • Product Delivered

    • When your product is delivered, update your order as delivered. The buyer has 24 hours to confirm receiving the order or it will be automatically be marked as "delivered". After that, you will have to wait for 7 days before the amount is sent to your RiraPay wallet which you can withdraw to your bank account immediately


  • Refund Process

    • When a buyer is disatisfied with a product due to a difference in description or quality, they have 7 days after the order has been marked "delivered". Buyer has to prove their assertion with an evidence; you have 24 hours after the request has been made to counter their request. If you feel the buyer is right, you can accept the request or renegotiate a new price. If you reject the request, you can escalate the issue to our dedicated resolution team who will go through both conversation and make a verdict within 24 hours of making request.
